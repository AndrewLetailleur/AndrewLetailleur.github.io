About me

Hey. My name is Andrew, and I'm a tinkerer.

Over a course of my time in studies, I have made games and written essays. With some of those papers having to do with narrative and research on videogames themselves.

I also have functional experience with programming. Both in HTML and JavaScript, along with C# via Unity, and Blueprint design through the Unreal Engine.

If I'm not programming, chances are I might be thinking of some story idea instead, as I also have a mild interest in writing.

Also, I doodle stuff.

A Brief History...

At an Early age...

Since childhood, I've had an on/off interest with thinking up on video-game mechanics. Ranging from combat systems to hypothetical gameplay.

Education truly kicked off, when I studied initially at Ayrshire College, and picked up when I continued my studies in the University of West Scotland. Initially dabbing in many fields within the creative industry, before focusing on gaining the skills to help program and create playable games.

Now I am...

A tinkerer. Want something from me? Send me a message when my contact page works. That said, outside writing, I am also an avid gamer, even if I'm more 'casual' on that end.
