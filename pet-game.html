Web Petz: A Pet Game!

Raise your very own virtual pet with a friend and play games together to raise their stats.

A group project, for a multiplayer game containing many mini-games. Developed using Phaser 3.

To hatch your pet, click on the egg until it starts to move side to side.
Pet data should save automatically.
Evolutions have been sped up in order to allow for faster playthrough and development; some features and pets couldn't be implemented.
Game Controls
Player 1: WASD to Move, Spacebar for Action.
Player 2: 8426 to Move, right insert for Action.
